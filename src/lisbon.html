Top Insider Tips

  • Ride tram 28 early to beat the crowds and see the city’s hills.
  • Sample pastéis de nata at a local bakery, not just the tourist spots.
  • Explore Alfama’s winding alleys for authentic fado music.
  • Catch sunset at Miradouro da Senhora do Monte.

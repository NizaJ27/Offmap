Tokyo

Tradition meets innovation in Japan’s vibrant capital.

All Destinations | Contact
What you can do in Tokyo: Dive into neon-lit neighborhoods, savor world-class sushi, explore tranquil gardens, and discover quirky pop culture. Tokyo is a city of contrasts—where ancient temples sit beside futuristic skyscrapers.

Tsukiji Outer Market

Location: 4 Chome-16-2 Tsukiji, Chuo City

What to expect: Fresh sushi breakfasts, bustling stalls, and a taste of Tokyo’s seafood culture. Try tamagoyaki (sweet omelet) and grilled seafood skewers.

Personal experience: I started my day with melt-in-your-mouth tuna and chatted with a vendor who’s been there for 40 years. The energy is contagious!

teamLab Planets

Location: 6 Chome-1-16 Toyosu, Koto City

What to expect: Immersive digital art installations that play with light, water, and your senses. It’s like stepping into another world—bring a camera!

Personal experience: Wading barefoot through a room of floating flowers was surreal. I left inspired and a little awestruck.

Yanaka Old Town

Location: Yanaka, Taito City

What to expect: Narrow streets, artisan shops, and a glimpse of Tokyo’s past. It’s a peaceful break from the city’s fast pace.

Personal experience: I found a tiny coffee shop run by a retired jazz musician—his stories and music made my afternoon.

Yamanote Line City Hopping

Location: Yamanote Line (loop train)

What to expect: Easy access to Shibuya, Shinjuku, Ueno, and more. Each stop is a new adventure—shopping, shrines, or street food.

Personal experience: I spent a day hopping off at random stops, discovering hidden ramen shops and quirky arcades. Tokyo rewards curiosity!

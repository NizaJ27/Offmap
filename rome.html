Rome

Ancient wonders, modern flavors, and local secrets in the Eternal City.

All Destinations | Contact
What you can do in Rome: Marvel at ancient ruins, savor authentic Roman cuisine, wander cobbled streets, and discover hidden piazzas. Rome is a living museum where every turn reveals a new story—perfect for history buffs, foodies, and romantics.

Colosseum at Dawn

Location: Piazza del Colosseo, 1

What to expect: Arrive early to beat the crowds and see the morning light on the ancient stones. The atmosphere is peaceful and awe-inspiring before the city wakes up.

Personal experience: Standing alone in the shadow of the Colosseum at sunrise, I felt the weight of centuries. It’s a moment I’ll never forget.

Trastevere Food Crawl

Location: Trastevere neighborhood (across the Tiber River)

What to expect: Sample classic Roman dishes like carbonara and supplì in lively trattorias. The area comes alive at night with music and laughter.

Personal experience: I joined locals for dinner in a tiny trattoria, and the carbonara was the best I’ve ever tasted. The owner even shared his secret recipe!

Jewish Ghetto & Hidden Bakeries

Location: Via del Portico d’Ottavia

What to expect: Discover a quieter side of Rome with rich history, ancient ruins, and bakeries serving sweet ricotta tarts.

Personal experience: I stumbled upon a bakery run by the same family for generations. Their ricotta tart was a revelation—don’t miss it!

Rooftop Aperitivo

Location: Rooftop bars near Piazza Navona or Pantheon

What to expect: Sip an Aperol Spritz as the sun sets over Rome’s domes and rooftops. The view is unforgettable and the vibe is pure dolce vita.

Personal experience: My favorite evening was spent on a rooftop, watching the city turn gold while chatting with new friends from around the world.
